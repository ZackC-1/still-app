Privacy Policy

Effective June 26, 2026 · Cadmus Labs LLC

Short version: the free app collects nothing and runs entirely on your device. Pro adds an account so your settings can sync across devices — we store only your blocking preferences and your email, never your browsing or the videos you watch.

1. What Still does

Still removes short-form video surfaces (Shorts, Reels, TikTok, and similar) on supported websites. All blocking happens locally in your browser. Still changes how supported pages look on your device; it does not record what you browse or watch.

2. Information we collect

  • Free tier — none. No account, no analytics, no tracking. Everything happens on your device and nothing is transmitted to us.
  • Pro (Still Sync) — only what's needed to sync. Cross-device sync requires an account, so we collect:
    • your email address — used only for passwordless sign-in (magic link) and essential account/purchase notices; and
    • your Still settings — which services you block, your paused sites, and on/off state. This is the only data we sync so your preferences follow you across devices.
    We do not collect your browsing history, the pages or videos you view, your location, or any content from the sites you visit.
  • Purchases. Payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or by Stripe via RevenueCat (web). We never receive or store your full payment-card details. We keep a record that your account is entitled to Pro, plus minimal purchase metadata (product, environment, expiration) needed to manage entitlement and support.

3. How we use information

Only to provide the service: to authenticate you, sync your settings, and grant or verify your Pro entitlement. We do not sell your data, serve ads, build advertising profiles, or share your data except with the service providers listed below.

4. Service providers

  • Supabase — authentication and storage of your email and settings.
  • RevenueCat — purchase and entitlement management.
  • Stripe — web payment processing.
  • Apple — App Store payment processing on Apple devices.
  • An email delivery provider — to send magic-link sign-in emails.

Each provider processes data only to perform its function for us.

5. Retention and your choices

You can export your data and delete your account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account removes your synced settings and entitlement record from our systems. You may also contact us to access, correct, or delete your data. We honor applicable rights under the CCPA and GDPR.

6. Security

Data in transit is encrypted with HTTPS/TLS. Stored data is access-restricted, and sync data is scoped so that only your own account can read or write it.

7. Children

Still is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; we will post the new effective date here.

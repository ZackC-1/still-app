Still — Support

Still removes short-form video — YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Facebook Reels — from the sites you visit, right in Safari on iPhone, iPad, and Mac, so you can use those sites without the endless scroll.

Getting started

  1. Install Still from the App Store.
  2. Turn on the Safari extension. On iPhone or iPad, open the Settings app → Safari → Extensions, turn on the Still extension, and allow it on the sites it needs. On Mac, open Safari → Settings → Extensions and do the same.
  3. Open the Still app to choose which services to clear and toggle them on or off.

Frequently asked questions

It's not working on a site.

A few quick things to check:

  • Make sure the Still extension is enabled in Safari settings and allowed on that site.
  • Check that Still is turned on in the app.
  • Make sure the site isn't paused (Still has a per-site pause), and that the service isn't toggled off.

What is Still Sync?

An optional one-time purchase — no subscription — that syncs your settings across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Buy it once on any device and it's unlocked everywhere.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap Get Still Sync, then Restore purchase.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app's signed-in screen, choose Delete account. This permanently deletes your account, settings, and purchase record from sync. You can also email us and we'll take care of it.

Does Still collect my data?

Still does not track you, show ads, or sell your data. It works only on the sites you choose. For the full details, see the privacy policy.

I signed in with Apple but it's not working.

Make sure you're online, then sign out and sign in again. If it still doesn't work, contact us.

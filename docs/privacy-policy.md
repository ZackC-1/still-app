# Still — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 25, 2026_

Still removes short-form video (YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Reels) from the
sites you visit. This policy explains what data Still collects, why, and your choices.

**Short version:** Still does **not** track you, show ads, or sell your data. The only data we handle
is what's needed to (a) sign you in and (b) sync your settings across your devices — and only if you
choose to create an account.

## What we collect

Still works fully **without an account** — if you never sign in, Still keeps your settings on your
device only and we collect nothing.

If you create an account to sync settings across devices, we process:

| Data | Why | Where it's stored |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Email address** | Account identity (from Sign in with Apple or a magic-link email) | Supabase (our backend) |
| **Account ID** | Links your devices to the same account so settings sync | Supabase |
| **Your Still settings** | The features you've turned on/off and per-site pauses, so they match on every device | Supabase |
| **Purchase record** | To unlock "Still Sync" (a one-time in-app purchase) and restore it on your devices | Apple + RevenueCat |

We do **not** collect: browsing history, the content of pages you visit, location, contacts, device
identifiers for tracking, or any analytics/advertising data.

## Service providers

We share the minimum data above only with the providers that make the app work:

- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple and in-app purchases (StoreKit).
- **Supabase** — stores your account and synced settings.
- **RevenueCat** — processes and verifies your in-app purchase.

We do not sell or share your data with anyone else, and none of this is used for advertising or
tracking.

## Your choices and rights

- **Delete your account** — In the app's signed-in screen, choose **Delete account**. This permanently
  deletes your account, settings, and purchase record from sync. (Your purchase still exists with Apple
  and can be restored if you make a new account.)
- **Export your data** — You can request a copy of your account data.
- **Use Still without an account** — Just don't sign in; everything stays on your device.

## Children's privacy

Still is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above.
